Plate App

Smart Plate — What it does

  • LED progress ring (24 px) shows 0–100% eaten in real time.
  • Status indicators on the LED display — power, Wi-Fi, and battery are shown via ring patterns/animations (no separate status lights).
  • Sensors estimate intake via weight/contact signals.
  • Wi-Fi connectivity for app pairing and data sync.
  • OTA firmware for wireless updates when available.

Privacy & Safety

Privacy (App & data)

  • Local-first design: meal logs and profiles are stored on your device by default. Optional sync is off unless you enable it.
  • Minimal network use: used for provisioning, optional updates, and features you start.
  • No medical use: educational info only; not a diagnosis.
  • Clear permissions: camera/photos, Wi-Fi, storage.
  • Data control: delete profiles or clear local data in Settings.
  • Contact: hello@bitebuddy.app

Safety (Hardware use)

  • No submersion: electronics are not waterproof.
  • Cleaning: power off → wipe with damp cloth & mild soap → dry fully.
  • Charging: standard 5V USB-C; avoid unsupported fast-charge.
  • Cables around kids: keep out of reach; supervise.
  • Heat & damage: stop using if swelling/odor/unusual heat.

Information above describes intended behavior. Details can vary by firmware/app version and model.

FAQ

Allergies — can the plate detect allergens?
The plate does not detect allergens. You can tag foods with common allergens and see reminders. Always check labels and follow clinician guidance.
Waterproofing — is it water-resistant?
The electronics unit is not for immersion/high-pressure water. Keep ports dry. If a removable food tray exists, follow its label for washing.
Cleaning — how should I clean it?
1) Power off & unplug. 2) Wipe with warm water + mild dish soap. 3) Rinse cloth & wipe again. 4) Dry completely. Avoid solvents/abrasives and spraying into ports.
Dishwasher — can I put it in?
Do not dishwash the electronics unit. Only a detachable tray labeled as dishwasher-safe may go on the top rack.
Charging — how long and how do I know?
Use a standard 5V USB-C charger. The LED display shows status (e.g., gentle pulse while charging; steady ring when full). Time varies by battery/adapter.
Is this a medical device?
No. bitebuddy provides educational feedback about eating progress/nutrition and does not diagnose, treat, or prevent disease.
